A Letter from the Founder

Antroinette Smith,
CMP, PMP®

For sixteen years, I produced events that carried someone else's logo. The boardrooms and ballrooms changed; the standard never did. Behind every flawless program was the same quiet discipline — and, more often than not, no name on the work. I preferred it that way.

The Artfullyours Agency is what happened when I stopped waiting for permission to build it my way: a boutique practice for the enterprise brands, agencies, and organizations that want the rigor of a PMP® and the eye of a creative director in the same person, on the same engagement — no junior hand-offs, no seams.

Every program still moves through four commitments I've refined over those years — Assess, Refine, Transform, Sustain — the A·R·T·S Framework™ beneath the polish. It's how excellence becomes repeatable instead of accidental.

But the work outgrew a single pair of hands. So I built the Creative Collective: a curated network of senior partners I assemble around each brief — strategy, sourcing, creative, communications — while you keep one accountable principal from first call to wrap report. One producer. Many masters. That's the next chapter, and I'd be glad to tell you where it's headed.

“My brand is my masterpiece.”

Artful in every layer. Strategic in every choice. Because the eye that sees the vision and the hand that builds it has always been mine.

— Antroinette Smith, CMP, PMP® · Founder, Principal Event Strategist & Producer

The First Master · Forefront Partner No. 01

Amy Parnell

Sourcing & Venue · Meeting Encore · Wexford, Ireland

Amy Parnell is The Artfullyours Agency’s forefront sourcing partner — the one who turns the right vision into the right spaces. Her hospitality career spans many leadership and strategic account management roles at renowned properties including the Sheraton Wall Centre, the Vancouver Marriott Pinnacle, JW Marriott Parq Vancouver, and The DOUGLAS, Autograph Collection.

Known for her relationship-first approach and meticulous attention to detail, she is fluent on both the client and supplier sides of the table, and known for navigating the most complex sourcing needs with results to match. Based in Wexford, Ireland, Amy partners with The Artfullyours Agency to support global accounts — sourcing venues, site selection, negotiating contracts, and helping execute meetings and events around the world.
