Introducing the Creative Collective

One Producer · Many Masters

This is not a pitch. It is an invitation — to the quiet craft of assembling the right people, and the belief that the work closest to you should be the most intentional.

By Antroinette Smith  ·  Summer 2026  ·  5 Min Read

Collective

kə·lek·tiv

Noun

a gathering of singular talents, chosen with intention, working as one.

I.  The Premise

A Boutique Agency Was Never One Person

There is a quiet myth about the boutique — one person, one pair of hands, one taste applied to everything. It is a beautiful story. It was simply never the whole one.

Behind every seamless room is a gathering of masters, chosen the way you would choose the scent that greets you at the door: deliberately, and for how it makes the whole feel. A boutique is not the absence of a team. It is the grace of never letting you feel one.

“The name on the door is one. The room behind it is many.”

II.  The Turn

When the Work Outgrew One Pair of Hands

A year ago, I left corporate to build The Artfullyours Agency on my own terms. Then the work grew — and with it the oldest question a founder is ever asked: keep pace by doing more, or build something that lets the standard hold.

I chose to hold the standard. Not more of me — more masters. People I trust at the very top of their disciplines, brought in by the brief, and held to the same quiet line I hold for myself.

“Growth, kept graceful, isn't more of me. It's more masters.”

Craft

kraft

Noun

Taste applied with discipline. The details that become the design.

III.  The Model

A Network, Assembled to the Brief

The Creative Collective is three things, kept as one. A curated network of named senior partners, each a master of their field. An unseen white-label bench that produces under your name and never steps into your light. And a vendor network, tended over years, that turns the right idea into the right room.

Nothing is assumed. Everything is chosen — strategy and production, sourcing and venue, creative direction and design, communications — assembled to the brief, and nothing beyond it.

One producer. Many masters.

IV.  The Principle

One Accountable Principal

For all the hands, you feel one. A single principal who signs the work, holds the timeline, and answers for every detail — from the first call to the last light switched off. That is the whole intention: the depth of a full house, the accountability of one person.

It is also, quietly, how a boutique outcreates the firms that outspend it — by gathering exactly the right masters, and nothing extra.

“You hire one principal. You inherit a collective.”

V.  The Invitation

Meet Them, One at a Time

This is the first of a series. In the issues to come, I'll introduce the Creative Collective the way it was built — one master at a time, each in their own words, each with the work that earned them a place in the room.

We begin the introductions with Amy Parnell, the Creative Collective's sourcing and venue partner — the one who finds the room.

The First Master · Forefront Partner No. 01

Amy Parnell

Sourcing & Venue · Meeting Encore · Wexford, Ireland

Amy Parnell is The Artfullyours Agency’s forefront sourcing partner — the one who turns the right vision into the right spaces. Her hospitality career spans many leadership and strategic account management roles at renowned properties including the Sheraton Wall Centre, the Vancouver Marriott Pinnacle, JW Marriott Parq Vancouver, and The DOUGLAS, Autograph Collection.

Known for her relationship-first approach and meticulous attention to detail, she is fluent on both the client and supplier sides of the table, and known for navigating the most complex sourcing needs with results to match. Based in Wexford, Ireland, Amy partners with The Artfullyours Agency to support global accounts — sourcing venues, site selection, negotiating contracts, and helping execute meetings and events around the world.
