Toni Grace at the Table

Welcome to the Table.

Blooms · Travertine · Santo Alabaster  |  Something Set

The table is where a house becomes a home. Toni Grace at the Table — our debut collection — gathers Santo alabaster, blooms, and travertine into settings made for the way you gather. Not perfection, but intention: the deliberate choosing of what stays, what you reach for, what holds an evening together. An invitation to slow down and set a more graceful table. By invitation only, Summer 2026.

Curated from The Collective & North Palm.

The Philosophy

Set the table the way she would.

Toni Grace & Co. is built around what fills a room and what your hands reach for — the objects that turn an ordinary evening into one worth remembering. Stone, bloom, and soft light, chosen for the table and the every-day ritual alike. Quiet. Considered. Beautiful.
